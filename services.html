A Best Spot For Chocolate

Chocolate is made from tropical Thebroma cacao tree seeds.
Its earliest use dates back to the Olmec civilization in mesoamerica.
After the European discovery of the Americas,chocolate become very popular in the wider world,and its demand exploded.
Chocolate is a food made from cacao beans.
It is used in many desserts like pudding,cakes,candy,ice cream,and Easter eggs.
It can be in a solid from like a candy bar or it can be in a liquid from like hot chocolate.
Commercial chocolate has sugar and sometimes milk added.
